Things that Affect Dreams

Dreams are a series of images and sensations experienced during sleep. A dream can last a few seconds or 20-30 minutes, A person is more likely to remember their dream of they are awakened during the REM (Rapid Eye Movement) stage of their sleep which is about 2 hours of the 8 that a person is asleep. Dreams are often bizarre and strange containing events that the dreamer recognises. The dreamer is unaware that they are dreaming except when they are 'lucid dreaming' this means that the dreamer is aware that they are dreaming and in extreme cases can even control the dream. Dreams have been a subject of philosophical and religious interest throughout history. During REM sleep, the release of the neurotransmitters norepinephrine, serotonin and histamine is completely suppressed.

Dreams are not just happy-go-merry occurrences. There are some things that will affect your ability to dream and the results of your dreams. Things that can affect your dreams include

Things That Affect Your Dreams The Effect
Depression and Anxiety Causes bad dreams and difficulty dreaming
SSRI - A drug for treating depression Can cause difficulty remembering dreams
Alcohol Addiction Causes your dreams to more commonly be upsetting
Addictive Drugs Less time spent dreaming and when you have a withdrawal, it causes your dreams to be stranger than usual
Insomnia You are able to remember your dreams easier, but is also linked to more stressful dreams
Sleep Apnea Bizarre and disturbing dreams
Restless Leg Syndrome Nightmares, nothing more to say
REM Behaviour Disorder Causes the paralysis that occurs during REM to not occur, and as such, if upsetting dreams occur, your body will still respond, causing violent behavior
